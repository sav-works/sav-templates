# 🚀 SavTemplates — Gumroad Launch Guide

## Step 1: Create Your Gumroad Account
1. Go to **https://gumroad.com**
2. Click **"Start selling"**
3. Sign up with your email (or Google/Apple account)
4. Go to **Settings → Payments** and connect your payout method (bank account or PayPal)
5. Done. No permits, no tarakhi, no paperwork.

## Step 2: Create Each Product

For each product below, click the **"+ Product"** button in Gumroad dashboard and paste the content.
